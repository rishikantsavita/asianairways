Our Hiring Process

We believe in a transparent, fair, and professional recruitment process. Every application is carefully reviewed to identify talented individuals who share our passion for aviation excellence and customer service.

01

Application Submission

Submit your application through our career portal along with your updated resume and supporting details.

02

Profile Screening

Our recruitment team reviews applications to assess qualifications, skills, and role suitability.

03

Interview Assessment

Qualified candidates participate in interviews to evaluate technical expertise and cultural fit.

04

Final Evaluation

Candidate performance is reviewed and final hiring decisions are made by the leadership team.

05

Offer & Onboarding

Successful candidates receive an offer and begin their exciting journey with Asian Airways.
