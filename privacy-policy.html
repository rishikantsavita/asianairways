Information We Collect

Asian Airways may collect various categories of information to provide aviation services, travel support, customer assistance, cargo operations, business communications, and website functionality. Personal Information

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, organization name, communication preferences, and contact details when you submit inquiries, service requests, partnership proposals, or customer support requests.

Travel & Service Information

Information related to travel assistance, aviation services, cargo requirements, operational support, bookings, transportation requests, and business service inquiries may be collected to facilitate requested services.

Technical Information

Technical information such as browser type, operating system, device information, pages visited, referral sources, session duration, and website interaction data may be collected to improve website performance and user experience.

Analytics & Usage Data

We may collect aggregated analytics information to understand visitor behavior, optimize website functionality, enhance navigation experiences, improve security measures, and support operational decision-making.
