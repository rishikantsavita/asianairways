Membership Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about the Asian Airways Membership Program and discover how you can maximize your travel benefits.

Who can join the Asian Airways Membership Program?

Anyone aged 18 years or above can become a member and start enjoying premium travel rewards and exclusive airline benefits.

How do I earn reward points?

Reward points are earned through flight bookings, hotel reservations, holiday packages and membership renewals.

Can I upgrade my membership later?

Yes. Members can upgrade from Silver to Gold or Platinum at any time and instantly unlock additional privileges.

Do reward points expire?

Reward points remain valid according to your membership tier and activity. Regular travel keeps your points active.
